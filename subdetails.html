Submission Process

Submit to Us!

Authors submit their work that meets Tiramisu Research's content scope, including research articles, research reports, thesis, and lecture notes. Click the button below to submit your study or work to us!

Initial Review

Our team reviews the submission to check if it meets basic requirements of the study or work. This includes, but not limited to:

  • Clear title and author information (full name, institute and active e-mail)
  • Submitted in proper and readable document file.
  • Submission within our content scope
  • Submission being author's original work
  • Not containing hate speech, misinformation, unethical research, and any content that violates academic integrity or ethical guidelines

If a submission fails to meet one of the basic requirements, it will be returned to the author with revision requests. The author may choose to correct the issues and resubmit their work for another review.

Second Review

The submission will undergo a more in-depth review to evaluate content quality. If necessary, the author will receive feedback for revisions before final approval.

Our editorial team will then adjusts the submission to match the platform's formatting guidelines. Minor language corrections may also be applied if needed.

  • Microsoft Word 2003 (doc)
  • Microsoft Word 2007 (docx)
  • OpenDocument (odt)
  • Rich Text (rtf)
  • Portable Document (pdf)

Finishing and Publication

The final version of the study or work will then be sent to the author for confirmation before publication. If the author agrees, the study or work is finalized and prepared for public access.

The study or work is officially published on the platform with a dedicated page. The study or work remains protected under a CC-BY license, allowing proper attribution.
