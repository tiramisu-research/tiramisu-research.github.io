About Tiramisu Research

Tiramisu Research is a research group that provides an open-access platform for everyone to share their studies and works with the world. Our goal is to make knowledge accessible, encourage collaboration, and help researchers gain recognition for their work.

Publish Studies and Works - You can submit research articles, research reports, theses, and even lecture notes to share with the global audience! Each submission receives a dedicated page where readers can access the study or work and supporting materials like documentation and datasets.

Editorial Support and Review - To maintain quality, we offer free optional editorial assistance to help refine submissions before publication. While we do not provide a formal peer review process, this service ensures clarity and proper formatting.

Community Feedback and Revisions - You can update your submissions anytime based on new findings or community feedback. This ensures that the study or work you published remains up-to-date and that you, as an author, have the flexibility to refine your work.

Copyright and Open Licensing - All works published on our platform are protected under the Creative Commons Attribution (CC BY) license, meaning you will retain ownership while ensuring your work remains freely accessible. If you decide to publish in an official publication, such as journals, we will remove your study or work upon request.

You can also join us as an editor, reviewer or developer to help improve the quality of submissions and provide valuable feedback. Whether you're passionate about academic publishing or community building, there's a place for you to contribute!
